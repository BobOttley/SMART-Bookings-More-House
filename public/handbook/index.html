Creating an Open Day Event

Before each Open Day, you must create the event in the system.
  1. Go to SMART Events & Tours tab
  2. Click Create New Event
  3. Fill in: Title (e.g., "January Open Morning"), Date & Time, Duration, Max Capacity
  4. Click Save

Families can now book this event through the website or you can book them in manually.

Creating a Taster Day

Same process - just select Taster Day as the type.

Viewing Bookings

  1. Go to SMART Dashboard tab
  2. See all bookings listed
  3. Filter by: Status (Pending, Confirmed, Checked In), Date, Type

Assigning a Tour Guide

Every tour needs a guide assigned.
  1. Open the booking details (click on it)
  2. Click Assign Guide button
  3. Select from the dropdown
System automatically sends:
  • Assignment notification email to the guide
  • Calendar invite (.ics file) attached

Scheduling a Private Tour

  1. Open the booking details
  2. Click Schedule Tour
  3. Select date and time
  4. Optionally assign a guide
  5. Click Confirm

The system sends confirmation to the family and calendar invite to the guide.

Checking Families In

CRITICAL: You must check families in when they arrive.

This enables:
  • Follow-up emails to be sent
  • Feedback collection
  • Analytics tracking

On the Day of an Open Day:

  1. Go to SMART Events & Tours
  2. Click on the event
  3. Click View Briefing Cards
  4. Find each family as they arrive
  5. Click Check In next to their name

For Private Tours:

  1. Go to SMART Dashboard
  2. Find the booking
  3. Click Check In button

Briefing Cards (For Tour Guides)

Before an Open Day, print briefing cards for your guides.

  1. Go to SMART Events & Tours
  2. Click on the event
  3. Click View Briefing Cards
  4. Cards are grouped by tour guide

Each card shows:

  • Family contact details
  • Daughter's name and interests
  • Special requirements
  • Notes from previous contact
  • Prospectus viewing history

Print or email to each guide before the event.

If They Don't Show Up

  1. Open the booking
  2. Click Mark No-Show
  3. System records timestamp
No-show follow-up email sent automatically (within 24 hours)

Declining a Booking (with Alternatives)

If a date doesn't work:

  1. Open the booking
  2. Click Decline Booking
  3. Enter reason
  4. Add alternative dates/times

Family receives email with link to accept an alternative.

Recording Outcomes

Track what happens after each tour.

  1. Open the booking
  2. Scroll to Outcome section
  3. Click Record Outcome
  4. Select: Interested, Applied, Enrolled, or Declined
  5. Add notes
  6. Save

This feeds into your conversion analytics.

Adding Notes to a Booking

  1. Open the booking details
  2. Click Add Note
  3. Type your observation

Note is saved with your name and timestamp. Visible to all admissions staff and shared with CRM.

Part 3: Tour Guide Management

Adding a New Tour Guide

  1. Go to SMART Tour Guides tab
  2. Click Add Guide
  3. Fill in: Name, Email, Phone, Type (Staff, Sixth Former, etc.)
  4. Check Active status
  5. Save

Removing a Tour Guide

  1. Go to SMART Tour Guides tab
  2. Click on the guide
  3. Uncheck Active
  4. Save
Don't delete - just deactivate so history is preserved.

  • 1 day before the tour

Tour Guide Feedback

After the tour, guides can submit observations:

  1. They click the feedback link in their assignment email
  2. They complete the form about: student engagement, family interest level, academic observations, pastoral fit notes
  3. You see their feedback on the booking details page

Part 5: The Enquiry Process

When a New Enquiry Arrives (Online)

First Thing in the Morning

When you arrive at 9am, check your daily task email and the CRM for new enquiries.

For each new enquiry:
  1. The prospectus has already been generated automatically - find the link in the CRM
  2. Email them the prospectus link immediately - this engages them straight away
  3. Call them (within 2 hours of the enquiry arriving)

When Someone Phones In

CRITICAL: You must log them in the system.

If someone phones to enquire, you MUST create an enquiry record while you're on the phone.

Go to: CRM → New Enquiry

Fill in these fields as you talk:

  • Parent/Guardian name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Daughter's name
  • Daughter's date of birth
  • Current school
  • Year of entry interested in
  • How did they hear about us?
  • Any additional notes / support needs
After completing the form:
The system automatically generates the prospectus link. Copy it and email it to them immediately.

The Personalised Prospectus

Send the link IMMEDIATELY after every first contact.
  • Online enquiries: The prospectus is generated automatically when they submit the form. Find the link in the CRM and email it to them.
  • Phone enquiries: When you create the enquiry in the CRM, the system generates the prospectus. Copy the link and email it.

Your job is to email them the link - the system generates the prospectus.

What to say in the email:

Subject: [Daughter's Name]'s personalised prospectus - More House

Dear [Parent Name],

Lovely to speak with you. Here's [Daughter's Name]'s personalised prospectus:

[LINK]

Looking forward to meeting you on [tour date].

Warm regards,
[Your Name]

Booking a Tour

On the First Call

Always book the tour while you're on the phone.

  1. Offer two specific dates within the next 5 working days
  2. Confirm which one works
  3. Book it in the system
The system will automatically send:
  • Booking confirmation (immediately)
  • Reminder (7 days before)
  • Reminder (1 day before)
You just need to book it in the system - the emails are automatic.

After the Tour: The 2-5-10 System

This is where YOU add the personal touch. The system emails them on Day 1 - you CALL them on Days 2, 5, and 10.

Day 1: System Sends Thank-You Email

You do nothing. The system automatically sends a thank-you email the day after their visit (if they were checked in).

Day 2: YOUR Phone Call

Your daily task email at 8am will remind you who needs a Day 2 call.

Hello [Name], it's [Your Name] from More House. I just wanted to check in after your visit. How are you feeling about things? Any questions I can help with?

Listen. This is a check-in, not a hard sell.

After the call: Mark the task as complete and add notes.

Day 5: YOUR Phone Call

Hello [Name], it's [Your Name] again. Just checking in to see how your thinking is going. Is there anything else that would help? Would a second visit or a chat with the Head be useful?

Day 10: YOUR Phone Call

Hello [Name], I wanted to touch base and understand where you are with your decision. Is there anything holding you back? What would help you feel confident?

After the call: Mark complete, add notes. Record the outcome in the booking platform.

When to Stop

Stop following up when:

  • They've submitted an application
  • They've said they're not proceeding
  • They've asked not to be contacted
  • They've confirmed a place elsewhere

Creating Enquiries (Phone Calls)

When someone phones in, you MUST create an enquiry in the CRM.
  1. Click Create Enquiry link
  2. Fill in family information: Parent name, email, phone
  3. Fill in student information: Daughter's name, age group, entry year
  4. Select interests and priorities
  5. Click Create Enquiry
On submission: System automatically generates personalised prospectus. Copy the link and email it to the parent immediately.

Enquiry Status Workflow

Status Flow:

New → Contacted → Interested → Following Up → Converted

Alternative paths:
Any status → On Hold (pause temporarily)
Any status → Rejected (not interested)
Any status → Archived (very old enquiries)

To Update Status:

  1. Click on the enquiry to open details
  2. Find the Status dropdown
  3. Select the new status
  4. Status updates automatically

Getting the Prospectus Link

The system generates the prospectus automatically. Your job is to email the link.

  1. Open the enquiry details
  2. Find the Prospectus Link section
  3. Click Copy Link button
  4. Paste into your email to the parent
If link not working: Try regenerating the prospectus, clear browser cache, or try a different browser.

Adding Notes

Record all communications and observations:

  1. Open the enquiry details
  2. Click Add Note
  3. Select note type (Communication, Follow-up, Observation, etc.)
  4. Type your note
  5. Click Save

Notes are visible to all admissions staff and include your name and timestamp.

Logging Phone Calls

Log every phone call in the system.
  1. Go to PHONE TRACKING or open the enquiry
  2. Click Log Call
  3. Select Call Type: Outbound, Inbound, Voicemail, Missed
  4. Select Status: Completed, No Answer, Callback Scheduled
  5. Add duration and notes
  6. Click Save

What to Record:

  • Topics discussed
  • Interest level
  • Any objections raised
  • Next steps agreed
  • Callback scheduled (date/time)

Part 9: Call Scripts

First Call Script (60 Seconds)

Opening:

Hello [Name], this is [Your Name] from More House School. Thank you for your enquiry about [Daughter's Name]. Do you have a quick moment?

Finding out more:

I'd love to understand a bit more about [Daughter's Name]...
  • What's prompted you to consider More House?
  • Are there any areas where she might need support?

(You already have year group from the enquiry form - no need to ask again)

Booking the tour:

I'd love to invite you for a tour. I have [Date 1] or [Date 2] - which works better?

Closing:

Perfect! You'll get a confirmation email shortly. I'll also send you the link to [Daughter's Name]'s personalised prospectus so you have a copy of it. Looking forward to meeting you!

Hello [Name], this is [Your Name] from More House School. Thank you for your enquiry about [Daughter's Name]. I'd love to have a chat. Please call me on 020 7235 2855, or I'll try again tomorrow. I've sent you an email with a personalised prospectus. Thanks!

Hello [Name], it's [Your Name] from More House. Just checking in after your visit on [day]. How are you feeling about things? Any questions?

Day 5 Call Script

Hello [Name], it's [Your Name] again. How's your thinking going? Is there anything else that would help - another visit, or a chat with the Head?

Day 10 Call Script

Hello [Name], I wanted to understand where you are with your decision. Is anything holding you back? What would help?

Handling Common Questions

"What makes you different?"

We're small by design - every girl is truly known. Class sizes are small, so your daughter gets individual attention.

"Is it expensive?"

I'll make sure you have the fee information. We also have bursaries - would you like details?

"My daughter has [specific need]"

Thank you for telling me. We have strong support for additional needs. Our SENCO can discuss this when you visit.

Troubleshooting

Can't log in?

  • Check CAPS LOCK
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try incognito mode
  • Use password reset

Enquiry not showing?

  • Check filters
  • Refresh the page
  • Search by name

Email not sending?

  • Check Settings → Email Configuration
  • Test connection
  • Check spam folder

Guide not receiving notification?

  • Verify guide email in Tour Guides list
  • Check if guide is marked as Active
  • Re-assign guide to resend

Can't see feedback?

  • Family must have been checked in
  • Family must have received and completed feedback form
  • Allow 24 hours after visit
